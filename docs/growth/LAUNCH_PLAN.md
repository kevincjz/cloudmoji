## Growth Channels (Ranked by Expected Impact)

### 1. Parent Word-of-Mouth (Organic — Highest ROI)
The share loop: Child says a word → Parent is delighted → Parent tells other parents
- Build sharing into the product (share voice recordings, share milestone cards)
- WhatsApp is the primary sharing channel in SG/APAC

### 2. TikTok/Instagram Reels (Viral Potential)
Content format: screen recordings of toddlers tapping emojis and saying words
- Parents LOVE sharing their kids' learning moments
- Multilingual angle is inherently interesting/shareable
- Hashtags: #toddlerlearning #bilingualkids #emojilearning #singaporekids

### 3. LinkedIn (Kevin's Platform)
Content angles:
- "My 2-year-old designed a language learning app"
- "How emoji keyboards are accidentally teaching toddlers vocabulary"
- "Building an APAC edtech product: lessons from Singapore"
- Links back to Epitome narrative (AI + human development)

### 4. Singapore Parent Communities
Key groups:
- KiasuParents.com forums
- Singapore Motherhood forums
- Facebook: Singapore Parents, Singapore Mummy Groups
- Telegram: SG Parents, SG Toddler Activities

### 5. Preschool Partnerships
- Approach 5-10 Singapore preschools for pilot
- Offer free institutional access for feedback
- Teachers become advocates
- Connects to Epitome's education vertical

### 6. App Store Optimization (see ASO.md)
- Keywords, screenshots, description optimization
- Apple editorial team outreach (kids category feature)

## Key Metrics to Track
- Downloads (daily, weekly, monthly)
- DAU / MAU (target 30% retention at Day 7)
- Free → Pro conversion rate (target 5%)
- Share rate (target: 10% of users share at least once)
- Average session length (target: 5+ minutes)
- Words heard per session (target: 15+)
- NPS from parents (target: 50+)
