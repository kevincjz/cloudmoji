# Content Marketing Strategy

## Brand Voice
**Tone**: Warm, confident, slightly irreverent. A tech-savvy parent talking to other parents — not a corporation.
**Persona**: Kevin as founder-parent, not "Cloudmoji the brand"

## Content Pillars

### 1. "Cloud Built This" (Origin / Authenticity)
The founding story is the marketing. A toddler typing emojis on a locked phone IS the product pitch.
- Video: Cloud tapping emojis and saying words
- Photo: Side-by-side of Cloud's iPhone emoji typing vs the app
- Thread: "My 2-year-old accidentally designed a language learning app"

### 2. "The Multilingual Edge" (Educational Value)
Position around Singapore's bilingual culture and the research on early language exposure.
- MIT research: language learning ability peaks before age 10
- Singapore context: 4 official languages, most families speak 2-3
- Practical tips: how to introduce a second language at home

### 3. "Building in Public" (Founder Journey)
Kevin's indie dev journey — resonates with tech/startup audience.
- Weekly build updates on LinkedIn/X
- Revenue milestones (transparent)
- Technical decisions explained simply
- Mistakes and pivots

### 4. "Parents Share Kids' Wins" (User-Generated)
The most powerful content is parents sharing their kids' moments.
- Repost parent videos (with permission)
- "Word of the Week" challenge
- Milestone celebration templates ("My child learned 100 words!")

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Audience | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| LinkedIn | Tech parents, educators, investors | Long-form posts, founder story | 3x/week |
| X/Twitter | Indie hackers, tech community | Build updates, milestones | Daily |
| TikTok | Young parents (25-35) | Kid videos, demos, tips | 3x/week |
| Instagram | Parents (broader) | Reels, stories, carousels | 3x/week |
| YouTube | SEO / evergreen | App tutorials, language tips | 1x/week |
