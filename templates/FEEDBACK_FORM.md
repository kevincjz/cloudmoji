# Parent Feedback Form — Template

## Purpose
Collect structured feedback from 20 test parents after 1 week of use. Directly measures H2 (parent value perception), H4 (sharing), H6 (bilingual wedge), and H9 (speech imitation).

## Setup
Create as a Google Form. Link: share via WhatsApp DM to each test parent individually (not in a group — personal feels more valued and gets higher response rate).

## Message to Send with Form Link
> Hey [name]! Thanks for trying Cloudmoji with [child's name] this week. Would love your honest feedback — just 5 questions, takes 2 minutes. Your input shapes what I build next. [Google Form link]

## Questions

### Q1: Usage Frequency
"How many times did [your child] use Cloudmoji this past week?"
- Never after the first time
- 1-2 times
- 3-5 times
- Almost every day

*Maps to: H2 (retention signal)*

### Q2: Vocabulary Transfer
"Did your child say any words they heard in the app — either while using it or at other times?"
- Yes (please share which words!)  [free text]
- No
- Not sure

*Maps to: H2 (learning value), H9 (speech imitation)*

### Q3: Screen Time Choice
"Would you choose Cloudmoji over YouTube/cartoons for 10 minutes of screen time?"
- Yes, definitely
- Maybe / sometimes
- Probably not
- No

*Maps to: H2 (value vs novelty)*

### Q4: The Bilingual Question
"How important is the Mandarin (中文) feature to you?"
- Essential — it's the main reason I'd use this
- Nice to have
- I only use English
- I haven't tried the Mandarin mode

*Maps to: H6 (bilingual wedge)*

### Q5: Sharing
"Did you share Cloudmoji with anyone else?"
- Yes — I sent the link to other parents
- Yes — I posted about it on social media
- No, but I mentioned it in conversation
- No

*Maps to: H4 (organic sharing)*

### Q6: Open Feedback
"In one sentence, what is Cloudmoji to you?"

[Free text — looking for "learning tool" vs "cute toy" language]

*Maps to: H2 (positioning perception)*

### Q7: Willingness to Pay (Optional — Early Signal)
"If a full version existed with more languages (Japanese, Malay, Spanish), progress tracking, and voice recording — would you pay $4.99 for it?"
- Yes
- Maybe
- No
- I'd want a subscription instead (e.g., $2.99/month)

*Maps to: H7 (early WTP signal — not a formal test yet)*

### Q8: Suggestions
"What one thing would make Cloudmoji better?"

[Free text — feature requests, bug reports, frustrations]
